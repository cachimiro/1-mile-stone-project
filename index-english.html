What We Do

The ability we have in handicrafts allows us to create beautiful accessories, including: Bracelets, Earrings and Necklaces. This opportunity is thanks to our two sponsors: Fundacion Conect Vision and volunteer Luis Pedraza, who provided training for creating the jewellery. We train the prospective employees, providing them with the skills they need to work with us, and once they have finished their training, we offer them a job. This gives them the crucial income they need to live and support themselves and is the first step towards a better, more sustainable life.
